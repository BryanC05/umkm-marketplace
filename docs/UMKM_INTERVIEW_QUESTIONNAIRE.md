# UMKM Marketplace Interview Questionnaires

## 🥗 For SELLERS (Food/Beverage Entrepreneurs)

### Section 1: Profile & Business
1. What is your business name and what do you sell?
2. How long have you been running this business?
3. Where do you primarily sell? (market stall, online, delivery, pickup only)
4. How many orders do you get per day on average?
5. What is your average daily/monthly revenue?

### Section 2: Current Challenges
6. What are your biggest challenges in running your business today?
7. How do you currently get new customers?
8. Do you use any apps or platforms to sell? Which ones?
9. What do you think is missing from the platforms you currently use?
10. How do you handle payments? Cash, transfer, QRIS, etc.?

### Section 3: Technology & Marketing
11. Do you use social media (Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp) for your business?
12. How do you advertise your products currently?
13. Would you be willing to pay a monthly fee (Rp 10.000/month) to list unlimited products and get better visibility?
14. What features would make your life easier as a seller?

### Section 4: Pickup vs Delivery
15. Do you offer pickup for customers? How does it work?
16. Do you use delivery services (Gojek, Grab, etc.)? What's the cost?
17. Would you prefer customers pick up orders at your location to save on delivery costs?

### Section 5: Pricing & Value
18. How much would you realistically pay per month for a platform that brings you more customers?
19. Would you prefer paying Rp 10.000/month or a commission per sale?
20. What would make the Rp 10.000/month membership worth it for you?

## 🛒 For BUYERS

### Section 1: Shopping Habits
1. How often do you buy food/beverages online per week?
2. What platforms do you usually use to order food? (Gojek, Grab, Shopee,Tokopedia, direct from seller)
3. What types of food do you usually order most?
4. Do you prefer delivery or pickup? Why?

### Section 2: Pain Points
5. What frustrates you most when ordering food online?
6. Have you ever had bad experiences with delivery (late, cold food, wrong order)?
7. Would you be interested in pickup options to save on delivery fees?
8. How much are you willing to pay for delivery fees? ( Rp 0-5k, 5-10k, 10-15k, doesn't matter)

### Section 3: Preferences
9. What information is most important when choosing a seller? (price, rating, distance, reviews)
10. Do you check seller ratings/reviews before ordering?
11. What would make you try a new seller?
12. Are you interested in deals/discounts from local sellers?

### Section 4: Technology
13. What app do you use most to discover new food sellers?
14. Would you use an app specifically for discovering local/warung-style food?
15. What features would make the app useful for you?
    - Discounts
    - Loyalty programs
    - Easy pickup scheduling
    - Reviews from friends
    - Other: ____

### Section 5: Willingness to Pay
16. Would you pay a small monthly fee (Rp 5.000-10.000) for exclusive deals from local sellers?
17. Would you prefer a membership model or pay-per-order?

## 💡 Tips for Interviewers

1. **Build rapport first** - Start with casual conversation
2. **Ask "why"** - Dig deeper into answers
3. **Don't suggest answers** - Let them speak their mind
4. **Take notes** - Write down exact quotes if possible
5. **Follow up** - Ask for clarification when needed
6. **Thank them** - Offer a small token of appreciation if appropriate

## 🎯 Key Metrics to Track

- **Sellers**: Conversion rate, revenue, platform adoption willingness
- **Buyers**: Order frequency, price sensitivity, feature preferences
- **General**: Pain points, willingness to pay, trust factors
